Setup Guidance

Onboarding a restaurant executes the following backend automation:

  • Creates a new database record under the Restaurant model.
  • Generates a unique login username (e.g. "akhan") based on the admin's name.
  • Generates a randomized secure temporary password.
  • Forces a password change on the administrator's first login.
The credentials will be displayed only once on the next screen. Please copy them securely.
